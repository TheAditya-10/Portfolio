# Restaurant Data Analytics & Cuisine Popularity Prediction

Restaurant analytics using 10,000+ rows of order data to predict cuisine popularity.

## Dataset
- Order history with timestamps and cuisine tags.
- Customer segments and repeat-visit signals.
- Seasonal and promotional metadata.

## Architecture
- Random Forest and XGBoost classifiers.
- Feature engineering for time-of-day and seasonality.
- KPI dashboards for decision support.

## Trade-offs
- Balanced model accuracy with explainability.
- Limited external signals to ensure data quality.
- Focused on operationally useful features.

## Why these choices
- Operations teams need fast, reliable insights.
- Tree-based models perform well on tabular data.
- Dashboards speed up menu and staffing decisions.
